'Menu Spud' is a mobile application where customer can enter the restaurant, scan the QRcode on the table, and start looking at the menu offer by the restaurant. It helps to solve the problem of outdated menu as well as saving cost (menu designer, printing, and many more). The restaurant can update their menu in real time. In addition, waiter could be use for better purpose rather than standing at the side of the table waiting for customer order. On the other hand, customer will enjoy the ordering process. They could look at review of previous customers and make decision base on other review. Of course, since they had made the order from their phone, they could also review it after billing. This makes the reviewing much more realistic as compared to food blogger.
